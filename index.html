About Us

Our vision is to make disciples, raise up leaders, and reach unreached people groups. Bethel Full Gospel Church is a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spreading the love of Christ. We welcome everyone to join us in worship, fellowship, and service.

Founded in 2006, our church has been a beacon of hope and faith in the community. We believe in the power of prayer, the importance of fellowship, and the transformative love of Jesus Christ.

Our mission is to create a welcoming environment where individuals and families can grow in their faith, build meaningful relationships, and serve the community. We offer a variety of programs and services to meet the spiritual needs of our congregation.

Join us every Sunday for uplifting worship services, engaging Bible studies, and impactful community outreach programs.

Our Journey

2006 - Church Founded

Bethel Full Gospel Church was established with a small group of believers.

2013 - New Building

We moved to our current location to accommodate our growing congregation.

2020 - Online Services

During the COVID pandemic, we began streaming our services online to help people join in worship and stay connected.

Church Founder

Pastor. YOGESH

Pastor Yogesh, the Founder and Pastor of Bethel Full Gospel Church, has been serving since 2005. He is passionate about spreading the Gospel and helping people grow in their faith.
